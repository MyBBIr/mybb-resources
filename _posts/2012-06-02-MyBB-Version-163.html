MyBB 1.6.3 was released on 17 April 2011.

MyBB 1.6.3 is a security release and is now available to download. It fixes 1 high risk vulnerability and 1 low risk vulnerability. MyBB 1.4.16 was released simultaneously, fixing the same issues for the MyBB 1.4 series.

More information on the MyBB Blog: http://blog.mybb.com/2011/04/17/mybb-1-6-3-and-1-4-16-security-update/
