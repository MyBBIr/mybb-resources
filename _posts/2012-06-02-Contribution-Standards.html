All contributions to the MyBB Wiki are expected to be of a certain quality, so the MyBB Wiki can be a high-quality resource for all MyBulletinBoard users.

  • Articles should be as accurate as possible. Ensure that any procedures and references to MyBB features that you provide are correct.
  • Articles must be written for the latest public release of MyBB.
  • Articles should be as well-written and easy to understand as possible. Be as clear as possible in your explanations.
  • For the time being, this Wiki will be written in English. Please use correct English spelling and grammar.

Please note that all contributions to MyBB Wiki may be edited, altered, or removed by other contributors. If you don't want your writing to be edited mercilessly, then don't submit it here.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact one of the MyBB Team members.
