MyBB 1.4.13 was released on 19 April 2010.

MyBB 1.4.13 is now available on the MyBB website and is a security update to MyBB 1.4.12. This release is to patch two regressions related to the security updates in MyBB 1.4.12.

More information on the MyBB Blog: http://blog.mybb.com/2010/04/19/mybb-1-4-13-released-security-patches-to-mybb-1-4-12/
