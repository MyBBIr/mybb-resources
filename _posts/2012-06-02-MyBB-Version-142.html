MyBB 1.4.2 was released on 17 September 2008.

MyBB 1.4.2 is a general maintenance release and a security update to the MyBB 1.4 series. It fixes a few high risk and several medium risk and low risk security vulnerabilities. We recommend everybody upgrades to this release immediately or patch their boards with the manual patching instructions below.

More information here: http://community.mybb.com/thread-37792.html

As of 12 October 2008, MyBB 1.4.2 is the first release of MyBB to be released under the GNU GPL v3 License, as Open Source software. For more information about the change to Open Source, please see the official announcement.
