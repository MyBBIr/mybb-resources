Release Candidate 4 is considered a stable major release of MyBB, even though it sported the "Release Candidate" tag. RC4 was released on 4 October 2004. By this time, MyBB sported many of the features seen in the Features list today. Although it was a stable release, there were a couple of significant but not critical bugs.

One of the major features included in this version is inline moderation. This allowed Forum Staff to easily select multiple threads or posts and moderate them in the same way all at once.
