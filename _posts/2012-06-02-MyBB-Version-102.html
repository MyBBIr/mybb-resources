The discovery of exploits affecting the MyBB 1.0 series has prompted us to release an important security update for MyBB.

Boards affected are as follows:

  • MyBB 1.0 Preview Release 2
  • MyBB 1.0 "Final"
  • MyBB 1.01

This small update also fixes a few MySQL 5.0 compatibility issues discovered in MyBB.

We urge all uses to update their boards as soon as possible.

Affected files: 

   * admin/moderate.php
   * admin/themes.php
   * inc/functions.php
   * inc/functions_upload.php
   * printthread.php
   * usercp.php

More information: http://community.mybb.com/showthread.php?tid=5852
